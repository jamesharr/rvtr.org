Park status update Oct 17 2011: Yes the river has finally dropped to its normal levels but unfortunately it has left us a huge mess to clean up. The park is all maintained by volunteer workers (The River Valley Trail Riders), we will be having a work day this sunday the 23rd. I am betting that we wont be able to do much but any one who would like to see what its going to take to get it back open is welcome to come. My estimate would be the end of next summer with the volunteers we have now and alot sooner if more the fans of the park would like to donate some of time here and there. For the DNR to let us re open the park will have to be secure (fenced) and safe to ride in.
