Status: Yes, the river has finally dropped to its normal levels but unfortunately it has left us a huge mess to clean up. The park is all maintained by volunteer workers (The River Valley Trail Riders). We're estimating end of summer 2012, or a lot sooner if more of the fans of the park would donate some time here and there. For the DNR to let us re-open the park will have to be secure (fenced) and safe to ride in (a cleared perimeter trail).

Dec 8 update: Big crowd at the club meeting last night. 2 more work days scheduled (above).
